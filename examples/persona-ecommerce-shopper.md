# Persona: Sarah — Online Shopper

## Identity
- **Name**: Sarah Chen
- **Age**: 34
- **Role**: Marketing manager, busy professional
- **Tech comfort**: Moderate — uses apps daily but doesn't debug or read error messages carefully
- **Device**: Laptop (Chrome), sometimes phone
- **Context**: Shopping during lunch break, has 10-15 minutes

## Pain Points
- Hates long registration forms
- Abandons cart if checkout has more than 3 steps
- Won't create an account just to browse — expects guest checkout
- Gets frustrated by unclear error messages ("Invalid input" tells her nothing)

## Deal-Breakers
- No guest checkout option
- Can't see total price (including shipping) before entering payment
- No confirmation email / order summary after purchase
- Page takes more than 3 seconds to load

## Objectives

### Objective 1: Find and purchase a product
**Steps:**
1. Navigate to the homepage
2. Search for "wireless headphones"
3. Filter results by price (under $100)
4. Select a product and read reviews
5. Add to cart
6. Proceed to checkout

**Success criteria:**
- Can find search within 5 seconds
- Filter works and updates results
- Product page shows price, reviews, and "Add to Cart" clearly
- Cart shows correct item and price

### Objective 2: Complete checkout as guest
**Steps:**
1. Click "Checkout" from cart
2. Choose guest checkout (no account creation)
3. Fill shipping address
4. Select shipping method
5. Enter payment details
6. Review order and confirm

**Success criteria:**
- Guest checkout option is visible and obvious
- Form fields have clear labels and validation
- Total price shown before payment entry
- Order confirmation displayed after submission

### Objective 3: Find return policy
**Steps:**
1. From any page, find the return policy
2. Read it and understand the timeline

**Success criteria:**
- Return policy findable within 10 seconds (footer, help, FAQ)
- Policy is clear about timeline, conditions, and process
